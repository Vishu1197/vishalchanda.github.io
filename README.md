## 🚀 About Virexa Analytics

**Virexa Analytics** is a research-focused platform delivering **advanced computational biology and scientific analysis services**.

We specialize in integrating:

* 🧬 Bioinformatics
* ⚛️ Molecular Modeling
* 🤖 Machine Learning

to accelerate **drug discovery, disease understanding, and data-driven research outcomes**.

## ⚡ Our Core Services

### 🧬 Bioinformatics & Transcriptomics

* RNA-seq & microarray analysis
* Differential gene expression (DESeq2, limma)
* Pathway & enrichment analysis
* Biological interpretation of datasets

### ⚛️ Molecular Modeling & Simulations
